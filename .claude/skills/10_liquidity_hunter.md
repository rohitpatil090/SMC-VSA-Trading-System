# Skill: Liquidity Analysis (Added)
- **BSL/SSL:** Buy-side and Sell-side liquidity sit above/below old highs and lows.
- **EQH/EQL:** Equal Highs/Lows are 'Retail Double Tops/Bottoms' and act as magnets for price.
- **Inducement:** The fake breakout designed to trap traders into the wrong direction before the real move.
