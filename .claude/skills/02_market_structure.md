# Skill: Advanced Market Structure (SMC)
- **BOS vs CHoCH:** Break of Structure (Continuation) must have a Body Close. Change of Character (Reversal) must happen at an HTF Point of Interest (POI).
- **Protected Levels:** A swing high/low is only protected if it successfully took out liquidity (Inducement) before breaking the structure.
- **Fractal Nature:** A 15m BOS is an internal structural shift within a 4H impulse move.
