# Skill: Supply & Demand (Order Blocks)
- **Order Block (OB):** The institutional footprint. Must show 'Imbalance' (Fair Value Gap) immediately after the candle.
- **Breaker Block:** A failed OB that price reclaimed. Acts as a high-probability 'Flip' zone.
- **Base Formation:** Identify 'Rally-Base-Rally' structures as high-quality re-entry points.
